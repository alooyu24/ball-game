## Abstract
Care Cub is a healthcare support system designed to enhance digital healthcare services in Bahrain by addressing usability and functionality gaps found in existing healthcare applications. The system focuses on improving patient experience through role-based access, flexible appointment management, and an advanced notification mechanism. By integrating patient-centered features with efficient doctor workflows, Care Cub aims to reduce missed appointments, improve communication, and support better healthcare outcomes. The project was developed as a Senior Project for the College of Information Technology at the University of Bahrain and follows standard software engineering methodologies.

---

## Chapter 1: Introduction
Digital healthcare platforms are essential for improving healthcare accessibility and efficiency. In Bahrain, current healthcare applications provide basic services; however, users still face challenges related to navigation, appointment flexibility, and communication. Care Cub was developed to overcome these limitations by offering a more structured, user-friendly, and scalable healthcare system.

---

## Chapter 2: Problem Statement
Existing healthcare applications suffer from several limitations:
- Lack of hospital navigation support
- Limited appointment rescheduling and cancellation options
- Weak notification and reminder systems
- No clear role-based separation between doctors and patients

These issues lead to inefficiencies, missed appointments, and reduced user satisfaction.

---

## Chapter 3: Project Objectives
The objectives of Care Cub are:
1. Implement role-based user accounts for doctors and patients
2. Provide a flexible appointment booking, rescheduling, and cancellation system
3. Develop an advanced notification system for reminders and updates
4. Enhance overall usability and system efficiency

---

## Chapter 4: Project Significance
Care Cub contributes to healthcare services by:
- Improving patient experience and accessibility
- Increasing patient compliance through reminders
- Enhancing efficiency for healthcare professionals
- Strengthening communication between doctors and patients
- Offering a scalable solution suitable for multiple hospitals
